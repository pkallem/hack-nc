How/What We Measured?

Temperature

Using a DHT11 Temperature and Humidity Module, we will be taking the temperature (in Fahrenheit) of the surrounding environment. We will be plotting this data to see temperature change over time.

Humidity

Using a DHT11 Temperature and Humidity Module, we will be taking the humidity (in percentage) of the surrounding environment. We will be plotting this data to see humidity change over time compared to temperature change.

Water Level

Using a Water Level Detection Sensor Module, we will be taking the water level of the specific plants. There will be a water level sensor inside the soil to see how much water is currently in the pot. We will be plotting this data to see temperature change over time.
